About me

I'm Tafari Beckford, a Cloud DevOps Engineer based in Jamaica, skilled in implementing and maintaining DevOps practices and tools to streamline software development processes. I have a solid understanding of the software development lifecycle (SDLC), version control, containerization, container orchestration, continuous integration/continuous deployment (CI/CD), Infrastructure as Code (IaC), and cloud computing.

My goal is to help organizations improve their development processes, scale, deploy and secure their applications efficiently. Alongside my technical skills, I possess excellent problem-solving and communication abilities, which enable me to work collaboratively with cross-functional teams and prioritize critical tasks. As a lifelong learner, I hold a B.Sc. in Computer Information Systems from Northern Caribbean University and continuously seek to expand and validate my knowledge through labs, certifications, and other means. I am excited to continue learning and growing in the field of cloud computing, and I am always eager to tackle new challenges and find creative solutions.
